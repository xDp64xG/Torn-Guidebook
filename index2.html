Beginner's Guide

  • Learn about making money, XP leveling, and gym training.
  • Choco Jumps for initial stat boosts.
  • Explore factions and their benefits.
  • Check out education and merits for quick progress.

Making Money

  • Get a job to start earning income.
  • Trading: Focus on items like Donator Packs, Xanax, and Stat Enhancers.
  • Passive income options like Torn City Bank and stocks.
  • Fly with a private island for quick profits.
  • Participate in flower and plushie trading for steady profits.
  • Consider company investments for long-term income.

XP Leveling

  • Focus on balanced stat training for effective combat.
  • Use faction perks and chains to gain more XP.
  • Follow guides to pick targets strategically.
  • Engage in missions and events to boost XP gains.

Gym Training

  • Start with Choco Jumps for beginners.
  • Join a Music Store company for +30% gym gains.
  • Use Happy Jumps for quick stat boosts once financially stable.
  • Invest in gym upgrades for long-term gains.

Misc Tips

  • Check cooldowns for drugs, boosters, and medical items.
  • Education courses unlock valuable perks (e.g., faster gym gains).
  • Spend merits wisely on upgrades like Nerve Bar and Critical Hit Rate.
  • Regularly check forums and guides for updates.
  • Build connections in the Torn community for advice and support.

Cooldowns

  • Drugs: Xanax (6-8 hours), Ecstasy (3-4 hours), Vicodin (4-6 hours).
  • Boosters: Candy (30 min), E DVD (6 hours).
  • Medical: Blood bags and medkits are vital for recovery.
  • Use cooldown management tools to plan activities efficiently.

Education

  • Sports Science: First course to unlock gym gain boosts.
  • Biology: Unlocks blood bags, the best healing items in-game.
  • Business Management: Essential for running profitable companies.
  • Math: Reduces ammo usage and enhances productivity.
  • General Studies: Provides balanced benefits across various activities.

Factions & Companies

  • Factions:
    • Provide perks like reduced travel costs, faster cooldowns, and stat bonuses.
    • Choose a faction that aligns with your goals (e.g., combat, crimes, or reviving).
    • Participate in wars and chains for rewards and camaraderie.
    • Check forums for active factions recruiting new members.
  • Companies:
    • Start with entry-level jobs to build working stats.
    • High-star companies offer powerful perks, like gym stat boosts or energy refills.
    • 10-star companies are ideal for long-term income and job benefits.
    • Look into company guides for tips on maximizing profits.

Crimes

  • Start with low-risk crimes to build Nerve Bar and crime experience.
  • Invest in Education and job perks to increase crime success rates.
  • Advance to high-reward crimes as stats and experience grow.
  • Specialize in specific crime types to maximize rewards.

Combat

  • Balance your stats to avoid weaknesses in battles.
  • Use temporary items (e.g., melatonin) for stat boosts during fights.
  • Join a faction to participate in wars and unlock additional combat bonuses.
  • Train in appropriate gyms to counter opponent stats effectively.
